Hyukoh is a South Korean band that is proving there is more to love about South Korean music than just bubbly K-pop. With such a unique and diverse sound, Hyukoh has quickly become a mainstream success.

The now four-man band was created by frontman Oh Hyuk in 2014, initially as a solo performer under the name Hyukoh. In 2015, they topped the pop charts with their number one hit "Comes and Goes" from their first EP 20.

They continue to see worldwide success today with the release of their latest album Through Love. For more information, check out their webpage .
